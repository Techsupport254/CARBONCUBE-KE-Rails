# Financial Marketing Playbook

## 1. Introduction
This playbook provides a financial structure and strategic guide for the Marketing and Sales teams. It outlines budgeting frameworks, key allocation categories, and metrics for measuring success, ensuring that every dollar spent contributes to the company's growth objectives.

## 2. Budgeting Frameworks

### Startup-Specific Allocation
For early-stage tech startups, marketing budgets often range significantly higher than established companies to capture market share.
*   **Recommendation**: Allocate a defined percentage of **projected revenue** (or raised capital) to marketing and sales.
*   **Focus**: Prioritize **Brand Awareness** and **User Acquisition** in the early phases.

## 3. Key Expenditure Categories

### Digital Marketing Strategy
*   **PPC (Pay-Per-Click)**: Google Ads for intent-based search; LinkedIn/Meta for demographic targeting.
*   **SEO Tools**: Ahrefs, SEMrush, Moz (Subscription costs).
*   **Social Media**: Sponsored posts, influencer partnerships.

### Content Production
*   **Creation**: Freelance writers, video editors, graphic designers.
*   **Distribution**: Email marketing tools (Mailchimp, HubSpot), social media scheduling tools.

### Public Relations (PR) & Brand
*   **Agencies**: Retainer fees for PR firms.
*   **Press Releases**: Distribution services (Business Wire, PR Newswire).
*   **Branding**: Logo design, brand guideline development.

### Events & Field Marketing
*   **Conferences**: Booth fees, travel, accommodation, swag/merchandise.
*   **Webinars**: Hosting platforms (Zoom Webinar, etc.), promotion.

### Sales Enablement
*   **Tools**: CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), Lead enrichment (ZoomInfo, Clearbit).
*   **Commissions**: Structure for sales team incentives.
*   **Training**: Sales coaching and workshops.
*   **Travel**: Client visits and on-site demos.

## 4. ROI & Performance Metrics

To justify the budget, tracking return on investment is non-negotiable.

*   **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: `Total Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired`. Aim for a healthy LTV:CAC ratio.
*   **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: Projected revenue from a customer over their entire relationship.
*   **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**: `Revenue from Ads / Cost of Ads`.
*   **Lead Velocity Rate**: Growth percentage of qualified leads month-over-month.

## 5. Governance & Compliance in Marketing
*   **GDPR/CCPA**: Ensure marketing data collection complies with privacy laws (cookie consent, opt-out mechanisms).
*   **Truth in Advertising**: Avoid misleading claims; adhere to FTC guidelines.
